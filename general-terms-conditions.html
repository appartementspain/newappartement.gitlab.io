General Terms & Conditions

Applicable to all bookings of holiday apartment La Casa Alegría. Valid from January 2025.

Article 1

The apartment

La Casa Alegría is a holiday apartment located in the modern complex Velapi II in Los Alcázares, on the Mar Menor, Murcia, Spain. The apartment has a surface area of 88 m² and accommodates a maximum of 6 persons.

The apartment features 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a fully equipped open kitchen, a spacious living and dining area, a sunny terrace and a private underground parking space. Guests also have access to the shared facilities of the complex, including an outdoor swimming pool with children's pool, whirlpool, landscaped gardens and a playground.

The apartment is exclusively intended for tourist and recreational purposes and may not be used as a permanent residence or for commercial activities.

Article 2

Check-in & check-out

  • Check-in is available from 3:00 PM on the day of arrival
  • Check-out must take place before 10:00 AM on the day of departure
  • Guests are required to indicate their expected arrival time via the guest registration form (see Article 7). This information is essential for coordinating the key handover with our local contact person
  • If you expect to arrive before 3:00 PM or after 10:00 PM, please contact us in advance at info@lacasa-alegria.com
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be possible on request, subject to availability

Article 3

Costs & payment

The total rental price, cleaning fee and check-in fee are stated in the booking confirmation. The following payment terms apply:

  • Deposit of 30% upon confirmation of the booking
  • Remaining 70% no later than 4 weeks before arrival
  • Payment via secure online booking or bank transfer

In the event of late payment, the owner reserves the right to cancel the booking.

Article 4

Security deposit

A security deposit of € 450.– is required upon arrival. This deposit will be refunded within 7 days after departure, following inspection of the apartment.

Any damage to the apartment, its inventory or the shared areas of the complex caused by the guest or their party will be deducted from the security deposit. If the damage exceeds the deposit amount, the guest is liable for the difference.

Article 7

Identity verification & registration obligation

Under Spanish law, the owner is required to register the identity details of all guests aged 14 and over with the Spanish authorities via SES.HOSPEDAJES (Ministerio del Interior) within 24 hours of arrival.

To make this possible, guests are required to submit the following details for all members of their party aged 14 and over at least 48 hours before arrival, using the online registration form at lacasa-alegria.com/guest-registration.html:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Nationality
  • Document type (passport or EU identity card) and document number
  • Document issue date
  • Full home address and contact details
  • Expected arrival time at the apartment (for key handover coordination)

A driving licence or any other document is not valid for this legal registration. The owner reserves the right to deny access to the apartment if the details have not been submitted in full and on time.

Registered personal data will be used exclusively for the legally required report to SES.HOSPEDAJES and will not be retained longer than required by law (3 years). The processing of personal data is subject to the GDPR.
